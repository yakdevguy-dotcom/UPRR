# UPRR

Schedule app



Android only at this time sorry!!



This is a very crude and simple app to help the engineers and family members of the Union Pacific Rail Road see a visual representation of which days they have off. This was a side project in a language I had never attempted and was made to impress and help my dad. Donations are Welcome! buymeacoffee.com/Yakdevguy


Instructions:
Click on the app_release file, then the 3 dots on the right to download to device. Note: you will have to allow 3rd party app download and installations so check with your device.
